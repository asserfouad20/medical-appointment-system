About MediCare

Your trusted medical appointment management system

Our Mission

MediCare is dedicated to simplifying healthcare access by providing an efficient and user-friendly platform for managing medical appointments. We connect patients with healthcare providers, making it easier to schedule, manage, and track medical consultations.

Our platform serves three key user groups: patients seeking medical care, doctors managing their schedules, and administrators overseeing the entire system. Each user benefits from features tailored to their specific needs.

Key Features

For Patients

  • Easy registration and login
  • Browse and search doctors
  • Book and manage appointments
  • Online payment options
  • Rate and review doctors

For Doctors

  • Manage availability
  • View daily schedule
  • Track appointments
  • Mark consultations complete
  • Patient information access

For Admins

  • Manage doctors
  • Oversee all appointments
  • Manage schedules
  • View system statistics
  • System administration
